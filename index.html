ES Guy

Weekly booklet on Frontend Development

TLDR ES Guy is a small frontend developer booklet published every Saturday at 7pm CET containing weekly News, Interesting Reads, Changelogs for major libraries or frameworks, Weekly TILs and Roadmap coverage, all personally written or rewritten by me to keep it to-the-point and concise.

Yet another Newsletter?

No, unlike others, I do not send some random links that you may never read. And even if you do read, you have to spend so much time doing that.

What makes ES Guy different?

Each newsletter is a small booklet having five sections. I personally write or rewrite the articles making them quick-reads and to-the-point while giving you all the details. You don't have to spend time on newshacker, reddit, medium or twitter trying not to miss anything

There are five sections in the newsletter. News which contains all the news for the week. Changelogs which gives you the details about the new releases and changes in the major libraries. Interesting Reads of the week which were being tossed around at any of the social networks or something that I read. Weekly TILs, things that I learnt or found worth sharing and the final section would be Frontend Roadmap Coverage, starting from HTML and going all the way to the end, week by week.
